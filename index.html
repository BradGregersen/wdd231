About Me

Hello, my name is Brad Gregersen. I live in Pocatello, Idaho with my wonderful eternal companion, Emily. We have been married for eight years and we were sealed in the Idaho Falls Temple. I am studying for a career in full stack software development. My hobbies include coding, outdoors activities like hiking, and swimming as well as fixing and repairing all manner of things. I was born and raised in California up until I moved to Pocatello in 2017. My ancestors originally settled Pocatello and a neighboring town called Grace back in the 1800s. My goal in life is to bring more good into this world than bad, I feel like if we all do that, the world will be in pretty good shape.
